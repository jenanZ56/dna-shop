Type of life insurance


loan insurance

Loan insurance is an amount that is deducted from the loan installment itself and secures payment to the bank or any loan donor in the event of death


The insurance will pay half of the loan amount

job insurance

Job insurance is insurance against the risks that may affect the employee in the job, and the employee gets the insurance amount in case he is exposed to one of these dangers



The amount of insurance depends on the number of years of work and the degree of risk in the job and Compensation amount 60%
