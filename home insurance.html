Home insurance


Types of home insurance


fire insurance


Fire insurance is to insure your home against fire from any aspect in return for a percentage taken from the insured according to the price of the house and its age. In the event of a fire, you will receive compensation from the insurance


Compensation will be based on the value of the damage by 50%

theft insurance


Home insurance against theft is that you pay an insurance amount to a company, and in the event that your valuables are stolen, you will get the insurance amount



Compensation will be based on the value of the items by 50%
